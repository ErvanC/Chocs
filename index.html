"ABOUT US"

We’ve been inventing, inspiring and investing in a nation of chocolate lovers for nearly 4 years. Delve into the company’s fascinating history and you’ll find a wealth of interesting facts and information on subjects including how the nation’s favourite chocolate was born, the Chocs family, packaging, and so much more!

Our story begins in 2022 along the asphalt roads of Jakarta. First established as a little shop in Jakarta in 2018, we currently have three branches which are in Jakarta, Bandung, and Sanur. We provide many variants of chocolate products, especially dark chocolate. The business grows really fast since CHocs only uses the best quality ingredients for every customer, such as high-quality cocoa Belgian Chocolate, aromatic Mediterranean Hazelnut, and the thick and rich Milk.
